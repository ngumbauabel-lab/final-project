The Solution to Food Waste

Hunger isn't caused by a lack of food, but a lack of access. Our project focuses on three pillars:

  • Logistics: Connecting the 1/3 of wasted food directly to families.
  • Localism: Reducing carbon footprints by sharing within a 5-mile radius.
  • Dignity: Providing a simple, anonymous way to request and offer support.
